SAAC-lete of the Week!

Week of December 10th: This week we are recognizing Cross Country's Senior Captain, Nina Sitarski as this week's SAAc-lete of the week! Nina has been a 4 year member of the squad and has personal bests of 21:00 in the 5k and 27:00 in the 6k. She is a team player and very dedicated to her teammates. Her coaches describe her as tough and determined. Nina finishes out her season strong and will be looking to make an impact on the outdoor track and field team in the Spring.
